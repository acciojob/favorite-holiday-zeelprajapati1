Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is known as the City of Love and is one of the most visited destinations in the world. It is famous for its iconic landmarks, rich culture, fashion, and exquisite cuisine.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower - The most recognizable landmark in Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum - Home to the famous Mona Lisa painting.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral - A stunning example of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées - A world-famous avenue for shopping and sightseeing.
  5. Sacré-Cœur - A beautiful basilica offering panoramic city views.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissant - A buttery and flaky French pastry.
  • Macarons - Delicious, colorful, and sweet almond meringue cookies.
  • Crêpes - Thin pancakes filled with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Ratatouille - A traditional French vegetable dish.
  • Baguette - A classic French bread with a crispy crust.
